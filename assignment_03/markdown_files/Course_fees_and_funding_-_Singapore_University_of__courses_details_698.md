### Course Fees and Funding

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Full course fee inclusive of prevailing GST** | **You pay**  S$4,360.00 |
| **SkillsFuture Course Fee subsidy (70%)**  * For Singapore Citizens < 40 years old * For Permanent Residents | **You pay**  S$1,308.00 |
