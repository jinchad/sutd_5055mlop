Programme Outline
=================

##### **Learning Objectives**

By the end of this course, learners can expect to:

* Identify and apply leadership styles and roles of the Chief Sustainability Officer to drive sustainable transformation within an organisation
* Design an organisation for sustainability by integrating sustainable practices across departments, operations, and supply chain
* Develop stakeholder engagement methods and a sustainable culture to embed sustainability in the organisation’s values, mission, and decision-making processes

##### Day 1

* Roles of the CSO
* CSO leadership styles and versatility
* Style and strategy differences across industry and function
* Exercise: Profiling model CSOs in your organisation’s industry
* Managing your sustainability team
* Influencing other C-suite members
* Driving the sustainability agenda across all levels of the organisation

##### Day 2

* Designing the organisation for sustainability
* Organisational structure and cross-functional collaboration
* Reporting lines and decision rights
* In-class Assignment 1: Identifying potential improvements in your organisation’s set-up
* Purview of the sustainability team
* Sustainability strategy integration
* Budgeting procedures

##### Day 3

* Stakeholder engagement methods
* Ensuring inclusiveness in decision-making processes
* Creating conducive internal communication channels
* Exercise: Pre-empting stakeholder concerns in your organisation
* Maintaining successful investor relations
* Public relations and crisis management
* Community outreach and engagement

##### Day 4

* Developing a sustainable culture
* Behavioural vs cultural change
* Combining bottom-up and top-down approaches
* In-class Assignment 2: Envisioning a successful culture of sustainability for your organisation
* Culture champions and behavioural nudges
* Incentive design and sustainability performance management
* Team Project briefing and preliminary planning

##### Day 5

* Team Project: Developing a roadmap for sustainable transformation

##### **Assessment**

* Assignments
* In-class participation
* Team Project

##### **Subject Credits**

Upon completion and satisfying the requirements of passing this course, learners will be awarded 12 subject credits.
