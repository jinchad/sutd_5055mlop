Programme outline
=================

##### **Learning Objectives**

By the end of this course, participants should be able to / can expect to:

* Gain an in-depth understanding of various scaled Agile frameworks and their application in real-world scenarios.
* Develop strong collaboration and alignment skills to foster effective communication and coordination across multiple Agile teams.
* Enhance Agile leadership capabilities to empower teams, facilitate decision-making, and drive continuous improvement at scale.
* Implement advanced Agile practices and techniques to optimize value delivery, manage dependencies, and ensure alignment with organizational goals.
* Lead Agile transformations and initiatives with confidence, guiding teams and stakeholders towards successful outcomes in complex Agile environments.

##### Day 1

* Definition of Scaling Agile
* Importance and urgency of scaling
* Philosophy of scaling
* Challenges of scaling
* Understanding patterns
* Anti patterns
* Frameworks and their relationship to pattern
* Finding and selecting patterns

##### Day 2

* Logistical scaling challenges
* Examples of applying patterns
* Identifying and prioritizing scaling challenges
* Using scaffolds to work in a safer place
* Organizational change management
* Example of Agile scaling models to agility approaches

##### **Assessment**

* Assessment – 20 MCQ (Virtual)
* No Scrum Alliance exam

By successfully finishing this course in tandem with the Advanced Certified Scrum Master and Certified Agile Scaling Practitioner 1 programs, you set the stage for acquiring the esteemed Professional Certificate in Agile Project & Product Practitioner from SUTD. This thorough certificate signifies your mastery as a project and product practitioner within Agile settings.
